About Us

GreenMail is a Email application that tries to inculcate eco-friendly emailing habits in our users and also let them monitor and see the change they are making in the world!

Making a Change with changing user Emailing Habits

We believe that simple changes done on a large scale can produce exceptional results. We strive to spread awareness of habits that can make emails more eco-friendly.

  • We like to say no to Email attachments
  • We like to opt out of promotional content and news letters
  • We love short mails without much boilerplate

Alone we are weak, together we are strong. Join us and let's make a change!
