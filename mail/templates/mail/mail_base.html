Here are some Eco-friendly Habits to make!

The right audience

Cc only relevant people. Blanket emails may waste people's time & attention.

The right content

Hyperlink files or information held online rather than adding an attachment.

Compact is Good

Reduce the size of emails by lowering the resolution and compressing images and avoid large HTML elements.

The perfect info

Check your emails thoroughly before sending them to ensure they contain all the necessary information. It avoids the need for a follow-up email.

The correct list

Regularly clean and maintain mailing lists. Unsuscribe from unnecessary news letters and promotional mailing lists.
